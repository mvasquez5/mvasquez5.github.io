Target Audience:

Adult people between 24 and 60 years old. People who likes news, sports, shopping, travel and tourism. Constantly looks for news on their smartphones from home and work.


Scenarios:

Check weather before going to bring children to school.

Check weather before going to a trip on weekend.

Check the weather before going to play soccer on weekend.

Check the weather before walk in the morning.

Check the weather for general info while reading the news.


Personas:

Fictional Name Alejandro Barbosa
Job title Sales Manager / Pepsico
Demographics:
  • 55 years old
  • Married
  • Father of two children
  • Grandfather of two children
  • Has a Ph.D. in Digital Marketing.
Enviroment

He is comfortable using a computer and refers to himself as an intermediate Internet user. He is connected all the time to Internet. He uses email extensively and uses the web about 6 hours during his work day.

When he will use the website

He will enter the website on mornings before go to walk. Also use it on weekends before going to the park with his grandchildren.

Where he will use the website:

He will use the website on his iPhone. Usually from home before going to walking on the morning.



Fictional Name Linda Holmes
major responsibilities Wife and Mother
Demographics:
  • 35 years old
  • Married
  • Mother of one child
Enviroment

She has a smartphone and is connected to Internet all the day. She uses Internet 4 hours a day. She brings her child to school on weekdays.

When he will use the website

She will use the website on mornings before going to bring her child to school. She also will look for weather info on weekends before she goes out with her family.

Where he will use the website:

He will use the website on his smartphone. Usually from home before going to bring her child to school.
